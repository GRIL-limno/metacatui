About the KNB

The Knowledge Network for Biocomplexity (KNB) is an international repository intended to facilitate ecological and environmental research on biocomplexity.

For scientists, the KNB is an efficient way to discover, access, interpret, integrate and analyze complex ecological data from a highly-distributed set of field stations, laboratories, research sites, and individual researchers.
